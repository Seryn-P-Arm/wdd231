Site Name

The Art Den
I chose this site name because I love art and want to incorporate mycoding skills into something that I would actually use in real life, beyond this class. And I have a pretty good idea on what I want to do with it. I also want the site to be and enjoyable, fun, comfortable experience for users.

Site Purpose

This site provides users the ability to make commissions for art work that will be done by me, their requests will be detailed and made into a form on a commissions page, and they will be able to view an art gallery page that has most or all of my artworks that I have done in the past to give them a chance to see if they like the styles. It will also provide information on the background and guidance on how how to make a commission, factors to consider, view promotions or merch, view sponsorships and collabs and request for collabs by signing up to join my Art Den as a partner, and their name and artist details will be displayed as a member in the site. They will be able to find more info in it by clicking a button.

Scenarios

I want to commission a custom piece of artwork, but I’m not sure where to start. What kind of details do I need to provide, and how long will it take to complete? I’m an artist interested in collaborating with Art Den. How can I join as a partner, and what benefits do I get by being part of the Art Den community?
